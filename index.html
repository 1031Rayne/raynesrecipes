About Rayne's Recipes

Rayne's Recipes is a collection of family recipes that are frequently made at gatherings. This site was created to allow friends and family to easily view recipes and allow for submissions of new recipes. If you have a recipe that you love that you would like to see on here, or have any suggestions to improve the site, please feel free to submit it to us here.
