Crab Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 lb crab meat
  • 16 oz package of cream cheese
  • 1 tsp of mustard
  • 1 tbsp if worcestershire sauce
  • minced onion and chives

Instructions

  1. Mix all together on stove top.
  2. Serve warm.
  3. Enjoy!
