Corn Pudding

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 can creamed corn
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Blend sugar, corn starch, and eggs. Mix well.
  2. Add corn and milk
  3. Pour into casserole dish. Dot with butter.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 1 to 1.5 hours
