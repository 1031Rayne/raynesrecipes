Pumpkin Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of sugar
  • 1 cup plus 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 16 oz can of pumpkin
  • 2.25 cups of flour
  • 1.25 tsp of baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp of baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1.5 tsp of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Grease and flour 3 loaf pans.
  2. In a large bowl beat the eggs. Add the sugarand beat well.
  3. Add the oil and pumpkin, mixing well.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients, and mix well.
  5. Fill the loaf pans equally.
  6. Bake at 350°f for 50 to 60 minutes
