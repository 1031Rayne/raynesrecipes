Monkey Bread

Ingredients

  • 18 frozen dinner rolls (preferably Rhoades brand)
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tsp od butterscotch pudding powder (cook and serve - not instant)
  • 1/2 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle pudding, cinnamon, and nuts in the bottom of a 13x9 baking pan.
  2. Place the frozen rolls on the pan
  3. Melt butter and sugar then pour over the top of the rolls.
  4. Cover with dish towel and place in oven (off) overnight
  5. In the morning, remove from oven and preheat oven to 350°f.
  6. Once preheated cook for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Flip out of the cokkie sheet.
