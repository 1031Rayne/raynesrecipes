Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 6 to 7 mashed bananas
  • 2.5 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 tsp of baking soda

Instructions

  1. Grease 2 large loaf pans.
  2. Cream butter with the sugar. Add the eggs and beat well.
  3. Add the mashed bananas, stirring to blend.
  4. Add remaining dry ingredients.
  5. Pour into greased floured loaf pans.
  6. Bake at 350°f for 50 to 60 minutes or until done. (can also do as muffins - cook 35 to 40 minutes)
