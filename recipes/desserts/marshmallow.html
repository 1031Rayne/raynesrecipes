Marshmallow Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 box of brownie mix
  • 1 bag of miniature marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 4 tbsp of cream or evaporated milk
  • 2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla

Instructions

  1. Prepare brownies according to package directions.
  2. Remove from oven and immediately drop marshmallows all over the top.
  3. Put in oven for 5 more minutes, then remove from oven and drizzle with chocolate icing.
  4. Melt butter. Add cream (or evaporated milk) and cocoa.
  5. Whisk until smooth.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Add powdered sugar and vanilla, Whisk until smooth.
