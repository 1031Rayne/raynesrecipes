Dessert Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 can of refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 1 package of cream cheese, softened (8 oz)
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • Assorted Fruits



Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°f
  2. Spread crescent dough on cookie sheet and pinch seams together.
  3. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until brown.
  4. Remove from oven and let cool completely
  5. Combine cream cheese and sugar until blended.
  6. Spread over cooled crust.Arrange fruit on cream cheese mixture.
  7. chill and cut into wedges or squares.
