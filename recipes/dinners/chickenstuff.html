Chicken and Stuffing Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chicken stuffing
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of chopped celery
  • 1 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 cans of cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 6 chicken breast halves
  • 1 tsp of chopped parsley
  • paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°f
  2. Prepare stuffing by package directions.
  3. Add vegetables. Combine soup. milk, and parsley. Set aside.
  4. Arrange stuffing accross center of dish and arrange chicken around it.
  5. Spoon a thick layer of soup mixture on each side of stuffing.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Stir sauce before serving.
