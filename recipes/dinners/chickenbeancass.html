Chicken and Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cans green beans, drained
  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked, and cubed
  • 1 can water chestnuts, sliced and drained
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup miracle whip
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Put beans in bottom of large casserole dish.
  2. Add chicken layer, then layer water chestnuts.
  3. Mix cream of chicken soup, miracle whip, and lemon juice. Pour over chicken micture.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese
  5. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
