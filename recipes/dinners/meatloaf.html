Mom's Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Dash of celery seed
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup ketchup

Instructions

  1. Combine ground beef, celery seed, onion, milk, bread crumbs, egg, and worcestershier sauce in a large bowl
  2. Shape into a ball and place into a baking pan.
  3. Combine the brown sugar and ketchup and pour over the top of the meatloaf.
  4. Bake at 350°f for 45 minutes.
