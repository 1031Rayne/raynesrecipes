Chili

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs ground beef
  • 1 regular onion
  • 2 handfuls of chopped peppers
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of stewed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of light red kidney beans
  • chili powder
  • 4 tbsp of brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Brown the meat, onions and peppers then put into crockpot.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and let simmer for 6 hours.
  3. Top with shredded cheese and/or sour cream.
  4. Enjoy!
